# Evertz 7x00 General Platform Group 2 - 7881IRD2-ATSC

The 7881IRD2 series is used to receive, demodulate, and decode digital ATSC/DVB-T/T2/ISDBTb/DVB-C/C2/DVB-S/S2 satellite signals.
