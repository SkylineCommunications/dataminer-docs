# Rooms Page

Includes tables for Rooms, Zones, Rows, and Desks. Selecting a room filters the subordinate tables accordingly.

Zones can be configured with Thermal Type (Warm/Cold), highlighted in red or blue on the Room Designer, and Cooling Capacity (kW). Usage percentage is calculated based on the configured Maximum Power Consumption on the Asset Classes of the Assets assigned to the Racks placed in this zone.
