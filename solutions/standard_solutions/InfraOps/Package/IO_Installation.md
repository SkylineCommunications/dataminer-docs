# Installing InfraOps

To install dataminer.InfraOps:

1. Look up the [InfraOps package](https://catalog.dataminer.services/details/240a14ca-64cf-488d-a039-85be17d19f00) in the DataMiner Catalog.

1. Confirm that you all the follow prerequisetes are meet:

    - DataMiner 10.5.9 or higher. In addition, the [GQI DxM](xref:GQI_DxM) must be installed.
    - [MediaOps](xref:MediaOps) 1.1.3-CU0 or higher.
    - [Web File Manager](https://catalog.dataminer.services/details/330649ca-c685-4e1c-a9a5-aeca6ca8b40f).

1. When all prerequisites are met, click the *Deploy* button.

   > [!TIP]
   > For more details on deploying items from the Catalog, see [Deploying a Catalog item to your system](xref:Deploying_a_catalog_item).

During the installation, the following steps will automatically be executed:

1. Prerequisite check.
1. Install/Update Automation scripts.
1. Install/Update applications.
1. Install/Update DOM definitions.
1. Initialize the system (fresh install).
1. Migration actions (if any).
1. Cleanup actions (if any).
