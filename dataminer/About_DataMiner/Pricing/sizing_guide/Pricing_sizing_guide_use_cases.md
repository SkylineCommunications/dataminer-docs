# Sizing guide: Use cases

Use case examples show how DataMiner services come together in a specific, real-world deployment. Rather than prescriptive guides, they are intended as reference points. Use them to understand how a particular technology or operational scenario maps to service consumption, and to cross-check your own estimates.

|----------|-------------------|
