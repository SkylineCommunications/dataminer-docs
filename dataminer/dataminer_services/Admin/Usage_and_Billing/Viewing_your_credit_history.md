# Viewing your credit history

The Credit History page provides a detailed record of all credit transactions for your organization. It allows you to track every change to your credit balance, including purchases, subscriptions, usage charges, and corrections, giving you complete transparency over your dataminer.services billing activity.

## Accessing Credit History

To access the Credit History page:

1. Navigate to the Billing page in the Admin app.
2. Click the **Credit history** button to view the detailed transaction log.

## Time Period Selection

Use the date selector at the top of the page to filter transactions by month. This allows you to review billing activity for specific time periods and analyze historical credit usage patterns.

## Credit Transactions Table

The **Credit transactions** table displays a chronological list of all transactions that affected your credit balance. Each transaction includes the following information:

### Subject

The **Subject** column describes the transaction.

### Category

The **Category** column uses color-coded badges to classify each transaction type:

- **Billing** (blue): Monthly usage charges for consumed services and resources.
- **Subscription** (purple): Charges related to active subscriptions or subscription renewals.
- **Order** (green): Credit purchases or additions to your account, typically resulting in positive balance changes.
- **Correction** (yellow): Adjustments made to correct billing errors or apply credits.

### Initiator

The **Initiator** column shows who or what initiated the transaction, typically:

- **User email**: For manual purchases or administrative adjustments.
- **Skyline Communications**: For system-generated transactions or support-initiated corrections.

### Timestamp

The **Timestamp** column displays the exact date and time when the transaction was processed, allowing you to track when balance changes occurred.

### Balance

The **Balance** column shows your total credit balance after the transaction was applied. This helps you understand how each transaction affected your overall available credits.

### Change

The **Change** column displays the credit amount added or deducted by the transaction:

- **Negative values**: Credits consumed or charged (displayed in regular text).
- **Positive values**: Credits added to your account (displayed in green).

## Searching and Filtering

You can search the transaction table using the search icon in the top-right corner to quickly find specific transactions by subject, category, or other attributes.
